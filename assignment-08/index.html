Xbox One Setup


Xbox One consoles can sometimes be a challenge to setup, I will teach you how you can easily setup your console in no time at all.


Step One

First, check the back of your Xbox One console. To the far left side, there is a double prong port, which you will plug in your power supply cord. After plugging this in, you can plug the other end of the power supply cord into a wall socket or power strip.

Step Two

Next, find the port on the back of your Xbox One console with the name "HDMI Out", which is right next to the power supply port. Using your HDMI cable, plug one end into this port, and looking at the back of your television, find a port called HDMI (1, 2, or 3), and plug the other end of the cable into this. This allows your Xbox One to display image and sound on your television when the input on your television is the same as what you plugged the cord into, be it HDMI 1, 2, or 3.

Step Three

Finally, now that you have your console on and displaying video and audio, you need to be able to control it. On your Xbox One controller, find the "X Jewel" on the face of the controller, and hold it down until it lights up. Next, on the top of the controller, find the button with 3 symbols next to it, and hold it down until it starts blinking rapidly. And finally, find the button with the same symbol on your Xbox One console (on the left side facing the front of the console), and hold that button down as well until the power button on the Xbox One begins blinking, and wait till the blinking stops. Congratulations! You are now ready to begin using your Xbox One console.
